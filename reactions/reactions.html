Welcome to Space Reactions!

How to play:

In this reaction game, your goal is to click on 10 points that appear on the screen. The points will show up one by one, and the timer will count how long it takes you to hit all 10. The faster you click them, the quicker your time will be. Try to be as fast as possible to get the best time!
